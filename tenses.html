Present Tenses

Tense Usage Example Signal Words
Simple Present General truths, habits, routines She reads every day. always, often, usually, every day
Present Continuous Actions happening now, temporary actions, future arrangements She is reading a book now. now, at the moment, currently, today
Present Perfect Actions that occurred at an unspecified time, experiences, changes over time She has read that book. already, yet, ever, never, just
Present Perfect Continuous Actions that started in the past and continue, emphasis on duration She has been reading for two hours. for, since, how long, all day

Past Tenses

Tense Usage Example Signal Words
Simple Past Completed actions in the past at a specific time She read a book yesterday. yesterday, last week, in 2010, ago
Past Continuous Ongoing action in the past, interrupted actions She was reading when I called. while, when, as
Past Perfect Action completed before another past action She had read the book before dinner. already, just, never, before, after
Past Perfect Continuous Duration before another past action, cause of something in the past She had been reading for an hour when he arrived. for, since, how long

Future Tenses

Tense Usage Example Signal Words
Simple Future Predictions, spontaneous decisions, promises She will read the book tomorrow. tomorrow, next week, in 2025
Future Continuous Ongoing action in the future, polite inquiries She will be reading at 8 PM. at this time tomorrow, at 8 PM
Future Perfect Action completed before a future time She will have read the book by Friday. by, by the time, before
Future Perfect Continuous Duration before a specific time in the future She will have been reading for two hours by then. for, by the time
